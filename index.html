Pyrodinium bahamense Growth

Description

This project investigates the B-vitamin requirements and bacterial interactions of the HAB-dinoflagellate Pyrodinium bahamense.

Objectives

  1. Characterize bacteria associated with a Pyrodinium bahamense isolate from Old Tampa Bay (OTB)
  2. Investigate P. bahamense’s ability to grow under B-vitamin limitation
  3. Investigate P. bahamense’s ability to recruit bacteria for B-vitamin supplementation

Raw Data

  • b12_levels.csv — low diversity isolate in 5 B12 concentrations
  • fluor.csv — low diversity isolate
  • OTB_1006.csv — co-cultured with OTB seawater bacteria
  • IRL_1006.csv — co-cultured with IRL seawater bacteria
  • 1003_1006.csv — co-cultured with IRL culture bacteria

Functions

  • plot_growthfunction.R — R function for plotting growth curves
